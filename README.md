# IPO-Valuation-Prospectus-Analysis-
End-to-end IPO analysis project evaluating a recently listed company using its prospectus (S-1/DRHP). The project covers business and market analysis, risk assessment, detailed financial statement analysis, and intrinsic valuation using DCF, Comparable Company Analysis (CCA), and precedent IPO benchmarks. Concludes with a data-driven investment recommendation on IPO pricing (overvalued/fair/undervalued), supported by clearly stated assumptions, tables, and visualizations. Designed to mirror real-world equity research and investment banking workflows.
